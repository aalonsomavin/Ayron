Compact status or category label. Use `soft` (default) for most cases, `dot` for live/connection status, `solid` sparingly for high emphasis.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Connected</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning">Syncing</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral" variant="outline">Postgres</Badge>
```

Notes: sentence case, one or two words. Reserve `danger` solid for errors only.
